Where It Started

Bron Technologies wasn't born in a boardroom. It was built by a ten-year Air Force veteran who spent a decade leading people, solving problems under pressure, and figuring out how to make things work with whatever was available. That discipline doesn't leave when the uniform comes off.

Why We Exist

Most small businesses get ignored by big agencies or overcharged by freelancers who disappear after delivery. We built Bron Technologies to fix that. Fast builds, flat rates, and someone who actually picks up the phone. No corporate runaround. No surprise invoices. Just work that gets done right.

Where We're Going

Website Services is just the beginning. We're actively building out drone services, the SmartRecall® platform, and a mobile applications division — each one designed to solve a real problem for real people. We're documenting the whole thing in real time. You're watching it get built.
